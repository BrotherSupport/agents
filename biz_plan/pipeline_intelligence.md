# Pipeline Intelligence (Pipeline Analyst)
*Focus: Qualification, Velocity, and Risk*

## 1. MEDDPICC for AI Consulting
*   **Metrics:** What is the specific $ value of the manual labor being replaced?
*   **Economic Buyer:** Is the CIO involved, or is this just a "Director of Innovation" with no budget?
*   **Decision Criteria:** Does the client have a defined AI Security Policy yet? (If not, *we* provide the template to influence the criteria).
*   **Champion:** Who is the internal engineer who wants this on their resume?

## 2. Pipeline Velocity Benchmarks
*   **Target Velocity:** $500 / Day per consultant.
*   **Sales Cycle Length:** 45 days (AI is fast-moving; deals that stall past 60 days are likely "innovation theater" and should be disqualified).
*   **Win Rate Target:** 35% (AI consulting is competitive; we win by qualifying hard early).

## 3. Risk Signal Dashboard
*   **Red Flag:** "Single-threaded engagement." If we are only talking to one 'AI Enthusiast' and not the business owner, the deal is 70% likely to fail.
*   **Risk Signal:** No data access provided within 7 days of POC start.
*   **Recommended Action:** "If data samples aren't received by Friday, move the deal to 'Nurture' and reallocate technical resources."
